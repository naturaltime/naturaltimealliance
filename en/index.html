Platform of the International Alliance for Natural Time

Several doctors, scientists, health advocates, and organizations have formed an international alliance to raise public awareness of the need for official observation of solar-based time zones that are naturally appropriate to each state.

read moreâ€¦

An overwhelming and growing body of evidence from scientific studies and real-world trials shows that living against our internal clocks (our human circadian rhythms) is both acutely and chronically harmful.

Misalignment with our internal clocks significantly increases rates of sleep deprivation, depression, metabolic disorders, cardiovascular disease, cancer, and reduced life expectancy. It disrupts traffic, education, and work with decreased performance and increased accidents. It escalates fuel consumption and energy waste. It disrupts some religious communities. It needlessly burdens healthcare systems, economies, and the environment.

Acute misalignment is forced on society through biannual clock changes in spring and autumn. Chronic misalignment is forced on society by keeping clocks set to longitudinally inappropriate time zones. Year-round observation in each region of the time zone that is closest to nature, as defined by the sun, solves both problems simply and easily.

We seek to provide citizens and lawmakers with complete, reliable information that is relevant and essential to the setting of civil clocks. We ask that governments recognize the vetted data, and that they choose permanent time zones that are most naturally aligned to the longitudinal location of each state.

It is a human right to live in a society that respects our natural biorhythms. Restoration or enactment of permanent and naturally appropriate time zones will improve quantity and quality of sleep. It will benefit mental health, physical health, and longevity, particularly for societyâ€™s most vulnerable individuals. It will improve safety and performance on roads, at school, and at work. It will reduce expenses, pollution, and climate change. It will be fair to religious communities. It will provide balanced and healthful enjoyment of sunlight in all seasons. It is vital aspect of a just and sustainable society.

We call on the European Union, its national authorities, and the federal, state, and provincial governments of the United States and of Canada, to consult the worldâ€™s leading researchers in circadian and environmental sciences, to acknowledge these researchersâ€™ findings formally, and to publish concrete plans for restoration or enactment of naturally appropriate time zones before March of 2021.
